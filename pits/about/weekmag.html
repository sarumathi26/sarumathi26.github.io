The Week Magazine

PITS Ranked As One Of The Best Colleges in INDIA by THE WEEK Magazine. Press Reports about Parisutham Institute of Technology and Science (PITS), Thanjavur being ranked by the Top Indian Magazine THE WEEK (Issue Dated 21st June 2015). THE WEEK released the Rank List of Best Colleges in India based on a National Educational Survey by India's Top Educational Research Surveyor HANSA; In which PITS was ranked as the 99th Best Private Engineering College in the Whole of India and 72nd rank in South India (Tamilnadu, Andhara, Karnataka, Kerala and Pondy State).

PITS was again ranked as one of finest engineering institutions in India for the second time in June 2016.Ranking was based on academics, environment, sports and games, activities, infrastructure and placements.

Parisutham Institute of Technology & Science, Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, India.
