Rules and Regulation

The main aim of the hostel is to provide an ideal setting for a good and healthy formation of the Mind, Body and Spirit. It aims to foster a long lasting friendship amoung all inmates and give them a sense of emotional and sentimental security and a long lasting love for the institution. It has always been proved that hostellers have a great deal of attachment for the institution than non-residents.

Hostel curriculum is designed to give a greater mind strength to channelize the potential and inner strength in each and every inmate. Happiness and a sense of belonging is the ultimate goal. The main concern of the hostel is character formation and self development. Your happiness will largely depend upon your will power to adhere to the rules that lead to a fruitful freedom and better understanding of values.
